A nonprofit for photographers

We're a small but growing group of Wisconsinites with a passion for photography, dedicated to helping and connecting freelancers, artists, and small photography outfits throughout the state

An invaluable set of resources

We want your business to thrive, so we offer everything from how-to guides, to free or low cost promotion. We also welcome our members to communicate with each other, sharing knowledge and resources.

Join the family

Almost everything we have to offer is free of charge, and there are no membership fees, endless emails, or major obligations to worry about. We know your time is sacred. Come be a part of an organization that, like you, is passionate about the art of photography.
