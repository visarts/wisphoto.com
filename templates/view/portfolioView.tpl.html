Who we are

Wisconsin Photography is an organization for photographers, by photographers.

About us

We started as a services business in 2012, but as much of our business turned into more media driven services (video, audio, digital design), and as we talked to fellow photographers and begin to grow a following on social media, we realized what a great need there was for a one-stop-source for resources for Wisconsin photographers.
In 2015 we started plans to shift from offering services to customers, to being a service for photographers, and the response has been incredible. As the community grows, so does the strength of the organization, and the richness of resources provided.
We want to help your business be successful, and we believe sharing resources and knowledge is the best way to do that. We are even planning a regular TV show on state-wide public access channels that will highlight individual photographers and their work (stay tuned for more on that).
Our services business, Portitude Media will eventually make commercials and radio ads available to Wisconsin Photography members at a reduced cost.
